Demon Head: Thunder on the Fields


Copenhagen based band Demon Head have released their sophomore album, Thunder on the Fields, via Swedish label The Sign Records. The seven tracks continue their penchant for dark, sinister sounding post-punk, The band takes a stark sonic vibe, while maintaining a sparse, intimate feel to the tracks. This is a group that have forged a unique sound and an original niche.  Stream and buy Thunder on the Fields at the link below.

The Bordellos: Underground Tape Vol. 6


FZB favorites The Bordellos are back with Underground Tape Vol. 6. The four track EP is a collection of radio sessions and unreleased tracks, which give us a more internal look at this prolific UK group. The songs are raw and edgy, as always for the Bordellos, and also features a duet with Dee Claw from American cult garage band Persian Claws. The Bordellos are always a good bet for enjoyable records. Stream and download Underground Tape Vol. 6 for free at the link below.

Inhalt: Part Time Punks Sessions

Part Time Punks Sessions is the new album from San Francisco based electronic trio Inhalt. This album had us from the very first note. The eight tracks channel all the best vibes from 80's new wave, while delivering an album that is so sonically interesting, it was almost like a drug that engulfed the senses in a totally immersive trip. If you're into new wave and synthpop, this album is as good as it gets and is an absolute must have. Watch the video for album tracks Walking On Glass, then stream and buy Part Time Punks Sessions at the link below.

Monday, April 10, 2017

Bottle Next: Bad Horses


Bad Horses is the debut full length from Lyon based indie rock duo Bottle Next. Released on French label Le Cri Du Charbon, the eleven tracks recall influences such as Cream. Rocking and driving, the band melds elements from alt-rock, metal and math rock into a potent blend that is both original and retro. No small feat. This is as solid a rock album as anyone would want and a must have. Watch the video for album track Choices, then stream and buy Bad Horses at the link below.

Friday, April 7, 2017

Whereswilder: Hotshot


Greek label, Inner Ear Records, brings us, Hotshot, the sophomore album from indie rock band Whereswilder. The vocals recall the sound of Stars, but this band is heavier, with elements of math rock and a nice, albeit quite derivative, retro feel. The guitars are definitely the standouts on this album, with a sterling melodic sense and strong chops. As a whole though, the album is solid, but yet unremarkable. Stream and buy Hotshot at the link below.

Thursday, April 6, 2017

Orations: Wych Elm


Wych Elm is the latest album from Buffalo, NY based Orations. The eleven tracks are completely compelling post-punk anthems. The first thing that will hit the listener is the absolutely huge vocals of Jess Collins, who's style is reminiscent of Florence Welch. The instrumentals are driving, with plenty of twang and you'll get one of the tightest drummers we've heard in a long time. Taken together, what you have is an album that is super strong from start to finish. Watch the video for album track Oh, The Horror!, then stream and buy Wych Elm at the link below.

North Atlantic Drift: Departures Vol.1


Departures Vol. 1 is the new album from Toronto's North Atlantic Drift. The duo of Mike Abercrombie and Brad Deschamps deliver over an hour of music across twelve tracks, that include two live tracks. The album features dark ambient textures with a pervasive atmospheric vibe. Lush, but not cluttered, the deep synthpads feel snug like a warm hug. Crisp production caps of this superlative album. Watch the videos for album tracks Dream Sequence and Pretense, then stream and buy Departures Vol. 1 at the link below.

Tuesday, April 4, 2017

David Douglas: Spectators Of The Universe


Dutch artist producer David Douglas is back with his sophomore full length, Spectators Of The Universe, on Amsterdam based label Atomnation. The ten tracks glide through synthpop and mild house electronica, with analogue pads, an abundance of melodic elements and some disco laced R&B vibes. More mainstream and less experimental, the album none the less showcases an accomplished producer finding a solid musical niche. Polished beats, cliche sounds and a light touch make this a radio ready album for those that want their music easy rather than adventurous. Stream and buy Spectators of the Universe at the link below.

Monday, April 3, 2017

The Pinkerton Raid: Tolerance Ends, Love Begins


Tolerance Ends, Love Begins is the third album from North Carolina indie rockers The Pinkerton Raid. With a sound similar to Edward Sharpe and the Magnetic Zeros, the nine tracks are zealous toe tappers that make you want to shake your fanny in pure enjoyment. Sultry, spontaneous and catchy, with striking vocals and instrumental arrangements, this is a band that needs to be on everyone's radar. Stream and buy Tolerance Ends, Love Begins at the link below.

Friday, March 31, 2017

Reptillians From Andromeda: Sonic Rabbit Hole


Small Bear Records is releassing Sonic Rabbit Hole, the debut EP from Istanbul post-punk band Reptillians From Andromeda. The five tracks are edgy, noise laced affairs that will appeal to listeners of bands like Vivian Girls, Wild Flag and Sleater-Kinney. This is a band best heard at 2:00am in a dark, smokey room, where the vibe is real and the volume is loud. This EP is a must have. Stream and buy Sonic Rabbit Hole at the link below.
