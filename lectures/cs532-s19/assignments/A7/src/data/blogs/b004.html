Monday, November 5, 2012

Colin Meloy & Carson Ellis' Wildwood.


I just finished reading Colin Meloy & Carson Ellis' Wildwood. It is a fantastic story about a girl who travels into a forbidden forest with her friend to save her kidnapped baby brother. The heroine likes to ride bikes and draw birds. All images by Carson Ellis and found on Design Sponge (check out their review of the book), unless otherwise noted.


The book is filled with beautiful illustrations by Carson Ellis, from small drawings to full page color spreads. I highly recommend you check it out.

Badger with a Rickshaw.


A scene from Wildwood.


I just found out that there is a sequel, too. Next on the reading list. Image via Harper Collins Childrens.
