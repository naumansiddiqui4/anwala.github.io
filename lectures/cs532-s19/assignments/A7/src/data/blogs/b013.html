Crazy Leaves (2014)

An Indie Rock Playlist for Fall

For well over a decade I've made an indie rock playlist that captures (at least to me) the ambiance of the four seasons. This is the first time I've shared my autumn mix.

All songs chosen based on their ability to conjure up feelings, images, rhythms, and moods of everything that comes with the fall.

Click play and enjoy!

"Hey" - The Pixies

Wow. Amazing what 25 years does to musical perspective.

Few gave The Pixies the credit they deserved through the '90's as bands like Nirvana, Pearl Jam, etc were the ones to become famous with the fast-slow, fast-slow "alternative" sound. Foundational songs like this one were amazing achievements in an era dominated by glam rock and Brit pop.
